Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a credit check required?

add
No. We believe everyone should have the opportunity to own land. We offer guaranteed owner financing with zero credit checks and no background checks.

How does the title transfer work?

add
Once your final payment is made, we prepare a Grant Deed (or Warranty Deed) and record it with the county. You receive the original recorded deed, giving you full legal ownership.

Can I visit the land before buying?

add
Absolutely! We provide the exact coordinates for every listing. We encourage all prospective buyers to visit their land and experience the property firsthand.

What is the money-back guarantee?

add
We offer a 30-day "no-questions-asked" money-back guarantee on your down payment. If you're not satisfied with the land for any reason, we'll refund your deposit.
